The Problem: Research Is Multi-Objective

Noisy Inputs
  • Macro series disagree on definitions, lag, and revisions
  • News is a high-noise channel and easily narrative-driven
  • Prices embed expectations; signals often cancel out
Hard Constraints
  • Return, drawdown, turnover, costs, and constraints coexist
  • Risk is not one number: tails, correlation, liquidity
  • Most importantly: the process must be auditable and repeatable
What the Agent Optimizes
It turns “idea → evidence → decision → risk → postmortem” into a traceable chain.

Frames: The Unit of Controlled Comparisons

What is a frame?
A decision card that bundles goals, constraints, regime, and evidence. It is both a reasoning unit and an experiment unit.
Why a graph?
Evidence is dependent and sometimes conflicting. A frame graph represents dependencies and makes “remove one edge and rerun” ablations easy.
Output is not one sentence
The output includes recommendations, uncertainty bands, key assumptions, and contingency branches when assumptions break.
